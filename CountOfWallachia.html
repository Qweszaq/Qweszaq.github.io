Play her out on range, dont run left side since her aggro is further than this.
Use W just before you hit her/she hits you. This way you should tank a bit of her pull and first attacks damage.
Good idea is to stay on the other side of tombs when attacking her, if she pulls you, you will get stuck in tomb instead of getting in her range.
Use your T to counter pull, even if she pulls you, you wont get stunned (theoretically, sometimes doesnt work).
