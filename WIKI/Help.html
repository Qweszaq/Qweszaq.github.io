Some help:

- Recommended for use in fullscreen.
- If u have weird positioning, press ctrl+"+" or ctrl+"-" to increase or decrease scaling. Optional is when "wings" branch touches bottom border, but not owerrides it.

Usable:

- Click on item branch in the left and click on item. In the right (In this window) will appear his stats and his recipe. Click on big icon and more info about this item will appear in the "More info" window. Same with his components.

Backgrounds:

- Now you can choose only from 3 backgrounds. You can find this option in "More info" window.
