Shooter on Ancient Ent


Your hero is completely terrible on Ent, so just try to at least hold some main principles. --I will state two of them since i myself rarely take part in Ent runs.
Make sure you use Comet Storm(F) at first or third phase, using it on second phase will result in your death most likely.
Try to use W for Rock/Storm, its cooldown is really short, and under its effect you wont get stunned by the spell.
